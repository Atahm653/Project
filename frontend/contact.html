Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect all user data. We never share your information without consent.

Can I use HeartGuard AI instead of seeing a doctor?

No, HeartGuard AI is designed to complement, not replace, professional medical care. Always consult with healthcare providers for medical advice.

We recommend taking the assessment every 3-6 months, or whenever there are significant changes in your health or lifestyle.
